The 6-Month Team Impact Journey

The most-requested thing we do with teams: three connected stages that turn a group of strong individuals into one team — with 1:1 strategic advisory running throughout.

  1. Diagnose

    Stakeholder intelligence, questionnaire and confidential interviews. We find out what's actually happening before a single session is designed.

  2. Reframe

    Bespoke, fully-facilitated workshops built from the findings, turning data into a shared identity and a narrative that holds under pressure.

  3. Embed

    We make it land and last. Socialisation, 1:1s with each leader and a legacy narrative that survives team changes.
